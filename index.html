About Me

Bringing experience, knowledge, professionalism and problem solving to every project.
Developing Amazing Things with Passion for over 30 years.

I was lucky enough to start my career at a time when I could see and be part of the evolution of computing and software development. I worked with the very first releases of Microsoft Windows, the first standardizations of the C++ language, the early versions of Microsoft SQL Server, and the first formal specifications of HTML.

This history gives me a vast amount of knowledge to draw upon, but it also gives me great insight into how technology can be applied while taking into account requirements, budgets, timelines, longevity, business models and ROI.

There is a lot more that goes into a software solution than just knowing how to program. I make it my mission to stay abreast of the latest technologies in software development, but I also put great emphasis on understanding the real needs, audience, business objectives and impact of any software solution I develop.

Let me show you what my experience and knowledge can bring to your project.

  • Architecture

    With a broad knowledge of technology, databases, environments and platforms I can consult and architect the right solution for today and tomorrow.

  • UX Design

    Well versed in many forms of design and development models I can take your project from concept to requirements, UX design, development and implementation.

  • Web Development

    From websites, web applications, frontends, web APIs, integrations, middleware, to microservices, I have worked with all aspects of modern web development.

  • Mobile Applications

    I can deliver world class cross platform mobile applications from simple apps to complex solutions utilizing web backends, cognitive services, AR/VR, etc.
