Single Sign-on Integrations

We have a vast amount of experience integrating with different web-based services and applications. One of the most common integrations we have performed is for the purpose of authentication in both web and mobile apps.

We have performed direct integrations with Active Directory Federation Services as well as SSO standards like SAML, OAuth and OpenID Connect. We are also experienced with performing SSO integrations with popular API account services like Facebook, Google, Apple, etc.
